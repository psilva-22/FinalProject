What I love about Brazil

I love Brazil because it feels like a place where joy lives in the everyday—whether it’s in the music that spills into the streets, the vibrant colors of the markets, or the warmth and openness of the people. There’s a sense of energy and celebration woven into the culture that’s impossible not to feel. I’m inspired by Brazil’s natural beauty, from its beaches and forests to its incredible biodiversity, and I appreciate the deep traditions, food, and family-centered values that make it feel so welcoming. Brazil feels alive in a way that stays with you, and that’s why it holds a special place in my heart.

Simple Brazilian Recipe

Brigadeiro

  1. Ingredients:
    • 1 Can of Condensed Milk
    • 1 Can of Creme de Leite
    • 2 tbs pf Cocoa Powder
    • Half of a Milk Chocolate Bar
    • 1 tbs of Butter
    • Splinkles of your choice
  2. How to Prepare:
      3. To prepare the brigadeiro, start by placing the condensed milk, heavy cream, butter, cocoa powder, and chopped milk chocolate into a saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly with a spatula to prevent it from sticking to the bottom, until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the pan, reaching the point where it can be rolled. Then, transfer the mixture to a buttered plate and let it cool completely. After that, lightly grease your hands, shape small balls with the cooled mixture, and roll each one in the sprinkles of your choice. Place the brigadeiros in paper cups and serve.
